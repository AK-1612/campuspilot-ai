# CampusPilot AI — User Stories

## User Story 1: First-Year Student (Fresher)

### Persona
A first-year student who is unfamiliar with the campus layout and is attending classes in different buildings for the first time.

### User Story
As a first-year student,
I want to quickly find my classroom, laboratory, or department office,
so that I can reach my destination on time without feeling lost or dependent on others for directions.

### Scenario
During the first few weeks of the semester, new students often struggle to locate classrooms and academic departments. CampusPilot AI should provide simple, accurate, and easy-to-follow directions, reducing confusion and improving the overall student experience.

---

## User Story 2: Wheelchair User

### Persona
A student, faculty member, or visitor who uses a wheelchair and requires accessible routes throughout the campus.

### User Story
As a wheelchair user,
I want navigation routes that automatically avoid stairs and inaccessible pathways,
so that I can travel independently and safely across campus.

### Scenario
A wheelchair user needs to move between buildings and floors without encountering physical barriers. The system should prioritize ramps, elevators, accessible entrances, and alternate routes whenever necessary.

---

## User Story 3: Visually Impaired Professor

### Persona
A faculty member with visual impairment who relies on audio guidance and assistive technologies for navigation.

### User Story
As a visually impaired professor,
I want voice-based navigation instructions and landmark-oriented guidance,
so that I can navigate the campus confidently without depending on visual maps.

### Scenario
A professor needs to travel between lecture halls, laboratories, and administrative offices throughout the day. The navigation system should provide audio-first guidance and support screen readers and accessibility tools.

---

## User Story 4: Campus Administrator

### Persona
A campus administrator responsible for maintaining campus infrastructure and ensuring accurate navigation information.

### User Story
As a campus administrator,
I want to update route conditions such as blocked pathways, maintenance activities, or elevator failures,
so that users always receive accurate and safe navigation instructions.

### Scenario
A corridor becomes inaccessible due to maintenance work. The administrator should be able to quickly update the system so affected routes are automatically recalculated for all users.

---

## User Story 5: Emergency User

### Persona
Any student, faculty member, visitor, or staff member facing an emergency situation on campus.

### User Story
As a user during an emergency,
I want immediate access to safe evacuation routes and SOS functionality,
so that I can reach safety or request assistance even when internet connectivity is unavailable.

### Scenario
During emergencies such as fire incidents, medical situations, or security threats, users require reliable navigation and communication support. CampusPilot AI should provide emergency routing and offline alert capabilities through the mesh network.
