## A. Accessibility standards & law

- **[WCAG21]** W3C — *Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1.*
  Defines the four POUR principles and the A/AA/AAA conformance levels.
  https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/
- **[WCAG22]** W3C — *Requirements for WCAG 2.2.* WCAG 2.2 became a W3C
  Recommendation on 5 Oct 2023; it is a backwards-compatible superset of 2.1.
  https://w3c.github.io/wcag/requirements/22/
- **[WCAG22-Summary]** Level Access — *WCAG 2.2 AA Summary & Checklist.*
  Plain-English summary of conformance levels and the new 2.2 criteria.
  https://www.levelaccess.com/blog/wcag-2-2-aa-summary-and-checklist-for-website-owners/
- **[ADA2026]** Navigine — *Accessible Indoor Navigation: 2026 ADA Compliance
  Guide.* Notes the April 2026 ADA deadline relevant to public universities and
  maps accessibility needs to the POUR framework.
  https://navigine.com/blog/accessible-indoor-navigation-the-2026-guide-to-compliance-and-universal-design/

## B. Accessible wayfinding & navigation research

- **[Springer-Mapping]** *Accessible wayfinding and navigation: a systematic
  mapping study*, Universal Access in the Information Society (Springer).
  Reviews 111 papers; only ~23% address both indoor and outdoor accessible
  navigation, and notes GPS is unreliable indoors.
  https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10209-021-00843-x
- **[INsite]** UC Irvine INsite — *Accessible Navigation.* Co-design research
  finding that route preferences are frequently *shared across* disabilities.
  https://insite.ics.uci.edu/projects/accessible-navigation
- **[Manduchi]** UC Santa Cruz — *New apps will enable safer indoor navigation
  for blind people* (Manduchi group, ACM Transactions on Accessible Computing).
  Indoor wayfinding, "safe return", and AI scene description where GPS fails.
  https://news.ucsc.edu/2024/10/manduchi-wayfinding-apps/

## D. Agentic AI

- **[IBM-Agentic]** IBM — *Agentic AI vs. Generative AI.* Defines agentic AI as
  autonomous, decision-focused systems built on LLMs + ML + NLP.
  https://www.ibm.com/think/topics/agentic-ai-vs-generative-ai
- **[arXiv-Agentic]** *Ask ChatGPT: Caveats and Mitigations for Individual Users
  of AI Chatbots* (arXiv:2508.10272), §2.5 — agentic AI as planning + tool-use +
  goal pursuit beyond prompt-response.
  https://arxiv.org/pdf/2508.10272
- **[AutomationAnywhere]** *What is Agentic AI?* — the Perception → Reasoning →
  Action → Memory loop.
  https://www.automationanywhere.com/rpa/agentic-ai
- **[Unified]** Unified Infotech — *Agentic AI vs Traditional Chatbots* —
  autonomy, planning, tool integration, memory.
  https://www.unifiedinfotech.net/blog/agentic-ai-vs-traditional-chatbots/
