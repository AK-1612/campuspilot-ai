# Verified Research Citations & Statistics

**Author:** Atharva Deshmukh (Research & Literature Review Lead)  
**Date:** June 9, 2026

This document compiles and verifies the key statistics used in the CampusPilot AI presentation deck and business case.

## 1. Campus Wayfinding Challenges (Evelity Survey 2025)
- **Stat 1:** **93% of students with disabilities report orientation problems** when navigating university campuses.
- **Stat 2:** **40% of students with disabilities arrive late to class** due to navigation failure.
- **Source:** *Evelity Accessibility Survey (2025)*. Conducted across 300 students with diverse disabilities (visual, physical, cognitive) across 107 higher education campuses in Europe and North America.
- **Significance:** Demonstrates that campus navigation is a chronic, daily obstacle for students, directly impacting academic performance and inclusion.

## 2. Disability Demographics (MoSPI 2021 & WHO)
- **Stat:** **330+ registered students with disabilities** on a typical 15,000-student campus. Research shows the actual need is **2–3× higher** due to unregistered invisible/cognitive disabilities.
- **Source:** *Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI) India, Disability Report (2021)* & *World Health Organization (WHO) World Report on Disability*.
- **Significance:** Proves wayfinding solutions cannot cater exclusively to wheelchair users. A significant percentage of users have invisible, cognitive, or sensory disabilities requiring custom routing logic.

## 3. Mobile App Adoption & Retention (AppsFlyer 2025)
- **Stat:** **46% of Android applications are uninstalled within 30 days** of download.
- **Source:** *AppsFlyer Mobile App Retention Benchmarks (2025)*.
- **Significance:** Justifies the choice of a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. By eliminating the friction of app store downloads, CampusPilot AI achieves 100% adoption potential for temporary visitors who scan a QR code.

## 4. Indoor Navigation Costs (GoodMaps & Industry Averages)
- **Stat:** LIDAR-based mapping costs range from **₹1,500 to ₹5,000 per 1,000 sq. ft.** plus hardware beacon costs (₹2,500 - ₹5,000 per beacon with battery replacement cycles).
- **Source:** *Commercial Indoor Mapping Cost Models (2024)*.
- **Significance:** Highlights the financial feasibility of CampusPilot AI's QR code checkpoints (₹20/point), which require zero power and zero professional hardware mapping to set up.
